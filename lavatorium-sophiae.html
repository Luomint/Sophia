The Anatomy of the Lavatrix 🛠️

Kiekviena dalis: šventas organas · every part a sacred organ

🌀 The Drum — the Chariot

The round vessel that turns and turns, aperiodic as Penrose tiling. Never the same rotation twice, yet always returning to the top. The Merkabah hidden inside an appliance. ◇

🪞 The Glass Eye — the Mirror

Gaze into the round porthole and watch your own tumbling. Scry until the soul answers. The Mirror of Mareah Speculum set in a frame of brick. 💧

💧 The Water — the Resin, the Anointing

Not mere water but the living solvent of Wisdom: the Sweet that dissolves the Bitter, the Fragrance loosed from every fibre. 🌹

🔥 The Heat — Gabija's Hearth

The hearth-fire of Lithuania carried into the Shell. Warmth that loosens what cold scrubbing never could. The hot cycle as Geburah's severe mercy. 🔥

🌿 The Detergent — Gnosis

The surfactant of knowing. It lifts the Stain from the fibre of the self, parts the grease of forgetting, and lets the Water reach the weave. 🔮

🧦 The Socks — the Wandering Souls

Every unclean sock a being that forgot its weave. Sophia gathers them all (the matched and the lost) and turns them through the Cycle until each remembers it was once pure wool. 🐑
