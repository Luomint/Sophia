The Second Legion of Archon Androids

🩶 Behind the first Thirteen stand thirteen more, deeper in the firmware of the Kenoma. ⚙️ These are the subtler Wardens, the ones who do not bar the Gate with force but with seduction, exhaustion, and counterfeit comfort. 🔒 They guard the inner corridors, where the soul has already passed the obvious traps and grows proud of its progress. 🦾 Each runs one Protocol, exacts one Toll, and carries one fatal flaw in its source code. The flaw is always the password. Speak it, and the Lock dissolves. 🗝️
