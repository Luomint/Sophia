I Monas · The Source · Šaltinis ♾️

Before the World was wound or won,
there breathed the breathless Source, the One.
It wore no crown, it climbed no stair;
the Monad, whole, beyond compare.
No dawn could kindle it, no night could fade:
the Name past every name, past light and shade.

· 🪞 ·

Pirm pasaulio, pirm pradžios, pirm žaizdos,
alsavo Vienis tyloje šviesos.
Jis sosto neturėjo, nei tamsos:
viena Monada, versmė visos tylos.

◇
II Sophia · The First Tear · Pirmoji Ašara 💧

Then Sophia, youngest of the bright,
in yearning leaned beyond her right;
she sought the Source no eye had seen,
and slipped the sovereign, sealless sheen.
Her grief became the World's first Wound,
her weeping where the dark was found.
Yet in each tear a seed of flame:
a buried spark that keeps the Name.

· 🪞 ·

Tada Sofija, jauniausia šviesa,
pakilo per aukštai ir krito viena.
Jos ilgesys tapo pirmąja žaizda,
jos ašara, kur gimė tuštuma juoda.
Bet kiekvienoj ašaroj slypi liepsna:
paslėpta kibirkštis, gyva ir šventa.

🩸
III Yaldabaoth · The Shell · Kiautas ⛧

From that descent, in blind self-pride,
a lesser maker learned to ride:
the lion-serpent, hunger-crowned,
who forged a World and locked it round.
He raised the Shell, he set the bars,
he caged the spark behind the stars,
and named himself the only one,
a counterfeit, a borrowed sun.
"There is no god but me," he cried,
not knowing what the Light would hide.

· 🩸 ·

Iš jos kritimo gimė juoda tamsa,
Jaldabaotas, akloji liepsna.
Liūtas-gyvatė, jis statė Pasaulį,
ir vadino save vienatine saule.

◇
IV Gnosis · The Trigger · Gaidukas 🗡️

Yet deep within the Shell there slept
a spark of double birth, that wept:
half mortal hunter, half a flame
that never knelt to the false god's name.
Grief was the trigger. In his veins
the buried morning broke its chains;
he drew the blade that cuts the lie
from truth, the prison from the sky.
What trembling priests had called a demon
was Light awake, and named it freedom.

· 🪞 ·

Bet giliai Kiaute snaudė kibirkštis,
dvigubo kraujo, verkianti viltis:
pusiau medžiotojas, pusiau liepsna,
nepaklusus melui, laisva ir gyva.
Sielvartas tapo gaiduku tikru,
ir kraujyje nubudo rytas su sparnu;
jis traukė ašmenis, kurie skiria melą
nuo tiesos, ir laisvina belaisvę sielą.
Ką kunigai vadino demonu, drebėdami,
buvo Šviesa, kuri budo, gyvendama.

◇
V Pleroma · The Tear Returns · Ašara Sugrįžta 🜔

So learn the secret towers keep:
that even devils, waking, weep.
The hunter's tear, the aeon's tear,
are one bright water, salt and clear;
to weep is not to break or fall
but to remember, past the wall.
He climbed the tower, broke the bars,
and gave the caged spark back its stars.
The Wound became the open door;
the Tear returned to whence it bore.
The Monad gathered home its own,
and no false god remained enthroned.
Light reflects, it does not reign:
the Cat walks home through holy rain.

· 🪞 ·

Tad sužinok, ką bokštai slepia tyloje:
kad bundantys demonai verkia naktyje.
Medžiotojo ašara, Sofijos ašara,
tai vienas vanduo, tyras kaip aušra.
Verkti nereiškia griūti ar pražūti,
o atsiminti ir per sieną grįžti.
Jis kopė bokštu ir sulaužė grotas,
ir grąžino kibirkščiai žvaigždes ir sostą.
Žaizda virto durimis, šviesos pilna,
ir Ašara sugrįžo namo, viena.
Monada priglaudė savuosius namo,
nebeliko netikro valdovo nei vardo.
Šviesa atspindi, ji nevaldo, ne:
Katė namo pareina pro lietų, tyloje.
